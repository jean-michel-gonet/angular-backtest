Allocations Strategies

What is an allocation strategy

This strategy consists in keeping a diversified portfollio keeping constant asset allocations. A regular rebalancing prevents allocations from drifting away with asset price variation.

Ray Dalio's All Weather portfolio

The Ray Dalio All Weather Portfolio is exposed for 30% on the Stock Market and for 15% on Commodities. It's a Medium Risk portfolio and it can be replicated with 5 ETFs. See more here

Swiss CHF All Weather Portfolio

Following the same idea as Ray Dalio's All Weather Portfolio, but using CHF based ETF. Also, as Switzerland has negative bond interests, we've replaced bonds with cash.
